About

Hello! I'm Ingrid Streitz, and I play a variety of pop/country music in Minneapolis and Iowa City (where I am from) & strive to help people relax and have a good time!

I enjoy covering a variety of genres and artists, including Taylor Swift, Jason Mraz, and Bob Dylan. I play pop music and classic songs everyone can enjoy. I often take song requests and have kids come up and sing with me if they are interested.
